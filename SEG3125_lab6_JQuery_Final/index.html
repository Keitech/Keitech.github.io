About Us

Physio Heals provides the leading physiotheraputic and rehabilitation to patients of all age groups. We are committed to ensure you feel stronger and healthier than you did yesterday after every step of your customized program.

Services

We offer a variety of services catered towards your needs.


Range of Motion (ROM)

Stiff joints? Our physio plans will help you regain full body movement effectively and efficiently.

Custom Knee Brace

Using the best technology in the market, we will help design and find the best brace for you.

Vestibular Rehab

We will help design a customized exercise plan for you to promote vestibular adaptation and substitution.

Sports Physio

Getting you back on the court and the field at full potential is our goal.

Post Surgical Rehab

After your surgical procedure, we will help increase your strength, endurance and flexibilty.

Geriatric Physio

We will prevent muscle deteriation while restoring function and activity levels.
